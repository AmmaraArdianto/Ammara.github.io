FLCL

FLCL aka FoolyCooly, is an old anime made in the early 2000s. Made by the same studio who contributed in Evangalien. It has themes about puberty and the meaning of many things but said in figurative ways. It's a wacky anime, that has a special place in my heart because the themes, teh animation, the characters, the development, and the other personal memories I had with it. It's one of those animes, that I will cherish forever.

Dungeon Meshi

Dungeon Meshi, is a unique take on fantasy dungeon genres. It explores multiple themes and also creates delcious meals that looks so edible through the screen. Not only that, but it expands more on the outside world. Every character has a unique design, and experiments with many characters. At first it seems very simple anime but as you go into it, you realize the ever expanding world and characters it has. Made by the same studio who is kinda besties of FLCL, made Panty and stockings with garterbelt, Gurren Laggan, AND MANY OTHER ANIMES THAT I LOVED.

Sanda

Sanda made by the same mangaka that produced beastars and others. Also the daugher of the creator of Baki. Sanda is a special anime, it has so many secrets and every dialogue has secret meaning into it. Allowing me to look back and every scene and theroize what would happen next, therozie how other characters feel for each other,or even about the school the students are confined into. It's made by Science Saru which made the anime Scott Pilgrim anime and Dandadan which are two shows that I loved dearly as well.
